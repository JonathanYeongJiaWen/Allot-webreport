Allot was built with our users in mind

Allot's primary features

memory
Dynamic scheduling

The asynchronous dynamic scheduling algorithm is a backend that boosts productivity while keeping the user’s welfare in mind. The smart workarounds will forever be sustained by constants and fueled by data.

settings
Reminders and tools

Notifications will be sent out periodically by the app to counter forgetfulness. Tools will be deployed based on subjects users are working on like our in-built scientific calculator.

lock
Restrictions

Restriction of access to distracting apps such as games and social media during work times. Exception includes tools of education, for the phone to be a tool of education instead of distraction.
